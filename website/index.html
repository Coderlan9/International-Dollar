About 5,000 years ago that the Mesopotamian people created the shekel,
which is considered the first known form of currency. Since then,
we have come a long way in our monetary system. From Gold and Silver
coins, to banknotes, to wire transfers and eventually to
cryptocurrencies. Bitcoin, the first cryptocurrency created in 2009,
has started a revolution, spawning a number of new coins based
on the same blockchain technology. Now in 2022 this project is
the next step in our fiscal evolution. With a simple majority based
voting algorithm, we believe that we can deliver the same security
as the Bitcoin Blockchain. You might ask how?          

Security measures to prevent any attacks from malicious organizations:

-IP block protection:                   
  We only allow 1 IP per /24 block (256 addresses). We put this
  restriction in place, so that you would need to purchase thousands
  of IP blocks, to gain any significant voting influence. Even if you
  were able to spend millions of dollars, you wouldn't be able
  to purchase enough, since they are already in the hands of the ISPs
  and actively in use.                

-Buffer period:                     
  Until 2025 we retain the network majority, to create a buffer period
  in our early stages to prevent somebody from taking over.

Large number of honest nodes:                
  When you download the program and create a wallet, a pop up
  will ask you if you want to join the node network, as a small UDP node.
  We have reason to believe that at least 20% of users join the network,
  this will create millions of honest nodes in the future.

No IPv6 nodes:                     
  Since IPv6 addresses are cheap and basically unlimited in number,
  we have decided to ban those from our network. In comparison,
  only 4.3 Billion IPv4 addresses are in existence.
